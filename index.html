FAQ

What most folks ask

What do you need from me to start?

Logo (or name), brand color (hex if you have it), hours, address, services, and 3–6 photos. That’s it. A quick 15‑minute fit check locks it in.

How do payments work?

Small deposit to reserve a weekend slot; balance on launch. Card payments via Stripe/Square. Receipts provided.

Can I add more pages later?

Yes—this is a foundation. We can add pages/sections later as separate mini‑projects.

Do you handle domains and email?

Yes, via the Custom Domain Setup add‑on (DNS, SSL, www/non‑www, and basic email forwarding).

Accessibility?

We follow sensible defaults: semantic HTML, alt text, color‑contrast checks, and keyboard‑navigable controls.
